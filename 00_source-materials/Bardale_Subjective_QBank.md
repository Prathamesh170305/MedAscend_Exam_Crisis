|---|---|---|

| Ch. 7 PM Changes → Body preservation for travel | **Ch. 28 Medicolegal Aspects of Embalming** | Embalming is a separate chapter in Bardale |
| Ch. 21 Artefacts | **Ch. 6 Medicolegal Autopsy** | Bardale has no separate Artefacts chapter; subsection of Ch. 6 |
| Ch. 10 Wounds → Torture | **Ch. 25 Human Rights, Torture and Medical Ethics** | Bardale has dedicated chapter for Torture |
| Ch. 23 Forensic Psychiatry → Drug addiction vs habituation | **Ch. 49 Drug Dependence and Abuse** | Bardale defines addiction/dependence/habituation in Ch. 49 |
| Ch. 15 Impotence/Sterility → ART, AI | **Ch. 22 Assisted Reproduction** | Bardale has dedicated chapter for Assisted Reproduction |
| Ch. 31/33 Drug Abuse → LSD rave question | **Ch. 49 Drug Dependence and Abuse** | LSD listed under Hallucinogens in Ch. 49 |
| Ch. 37 Misc Poisons → Snake bite | **Ch. 38 Organic Irritants: Animal Bites and Stings** | Dedicated chapter |
