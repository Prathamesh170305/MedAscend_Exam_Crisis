## 🎯 Chapter 1 — High-Yield Pickups

1. **Perjury → §229 BNS** (old §193 IPC) — up to 7 years + fine.
2. **Dying declaration → BSA §26** (old IEA §32) — no oath, no cross-exam, valid only if declarant dies.
3. **Expert opinion → BSA §39** (old IEA §45).
4. **Police inquest → §194 BNSS** (old §174 CrPC).
5. **Magistrate inquest → §196 BNSS** (old §176 CrPC). Mandatory in custodial / dowry / exhumation / psych-hospital deaths.
6. **Coroner inquest abolished** in Mumbai on **29 July 1999**.
7. **Conduct money** — civil cases only.
8. **Leading questions allowed in cross-examination only** (and from the judge).
9. **Dying deposition > Dying declaration** in evidentiary value (oath + cross-exam).
10. **Doctor in court = Expert witness**.
