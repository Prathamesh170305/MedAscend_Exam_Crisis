# LP-08 — Diagram-Specific Prerequisites

| # | Term | Plain meaning |
|---|------|----------------|
| 1 | **Leading question** | Question that suggests its own answer (e.g., "You were present, weren't you?"). |
| 2 | **Hypothetical question** | Question based on assumed facts, used to elicit an expert's opinion. |
| 3 | **Compound question** | A single question that bundles two or more queries — confusing for the witness. |
| 4 | **Indecent / Scandalous question** | Question on intimate or shameful matter unrelated to the case — judge may forbid. |
| 5 | **Insulting / Annoying question** | Question meant to insult or harass — judge may disallow. |
