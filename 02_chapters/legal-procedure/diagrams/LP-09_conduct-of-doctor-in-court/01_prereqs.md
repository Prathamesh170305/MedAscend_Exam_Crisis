# LP-09 — Diagram-Specific Prerequisites

| # | Term | Plain meaning |
|---|------|----------------|
| 1 | **Professional secrecy** | Doctor's duty to keep his patient's information confidential — overridden only by patient consent, court order, or statutory duty. |
| 2 | **Contempt of court** | Wilful disobedience or disrespect to a court — punishable. |
| 3 | **Perjury** | The offence of giving false evidence under oath — punishable under §229 BNS (old §193 IPC). |
| 4 | **Composure** | Maintaining a calm, professional demeanour under cross-examination pressure. |
