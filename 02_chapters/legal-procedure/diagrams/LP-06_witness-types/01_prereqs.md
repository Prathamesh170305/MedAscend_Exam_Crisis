# LP-06 — Diagram-Specific Prerequisites

| # | Term | Plain meaning |
|---|------|----------------|
| 1 | **Common witness** | A witness who deposes about facts seen, heard or perceived by himself — also called witness of fact. |
| 2 | **Expert witness** | A person specially skilled in a subject (e.g., a doctor) who is permitted to give an opinion before the court. |
| 3 | **Hostile witness** | A witness who, after being called, gives evidence contrary to the case of the party that summoned him. |
| 4 | **Skilled witness** | Same as expert witness — used interchangeably. |
