# LP-05 — Diagram-Specific Prerequisites

| # | Term | Plain meaning |
|---|------|----------------|
| 1 | **Medical certificate** | A written statement issued by an RMP regarding birth, death, age, illness, fitness or vaccination. |
| 2 | **Medicolegal report (MLR)** | A written report prepared by a doctor on a person referred by police for a medicolegal purpose (injury, sexual assault, intoxication). |
| 3 | **Dying declaration** | A statement made by a person who is dying, regarding the cause and circumstances of his impending death. |
| 4 | **Dying deposition** | A statement of a dying person recorded in the form of court deposition — in the presence of a magistrate, on oath, with cross-examination. |
| 5 | **Res gestae** | Statements forming part of the same transaction, made spontaneously enough that fabrication is unlikely — admissible despite being hearsay. |
