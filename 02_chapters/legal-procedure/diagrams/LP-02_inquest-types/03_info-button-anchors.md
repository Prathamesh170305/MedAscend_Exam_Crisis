### NODE: Central banner — "INQUEST = Legal inquiry to ascertain cause of death"
- **Start sentence:** "An inquest is a legal inquiry or investigation to ascertain the circumstances and cause of death."
- **End sentence:** "Only Police and Magistrate's inquest are held in India."

### NODE: POLICE INQUEST
- **Start sentence:** "The inquest is held under section 174 of CrPC."
- **End sentence:** "At that place, the IO in presence of two or more responsible persons of the area (called as Panchas) makes an investigation and prepares a report called as Panchnama."
<!-- Backend source: Source B p.3-4 (Police Inquest section); current law: CrPC §174 → BNSS §194 -->

### NODE: MAGISTRATE INQUEST
- **Start sentence:** "Magistrate inquest is held under section 176 of CrPC."
- **End sentence:** "It is mandatory in cases of death in police custody, dowry death (within seven years of marriage), exhumation, and death in psychiatric hospitals."
<!-- Backend source: Source A — Magistrate Inquest; Source B p.4; current law: CrPC §176 → BNSS §196 -->
