# LP-02 — Diagram-Specific Prerequisites

| # | Term | Plain meaning |
|---|------|----------------|
| 1 | **Investigating Officer (IO)** | The police officer (usually ≥ rank of Sub-Inspector) who conducts a police inquest. |
| 2 | **Panchas** | Two or more responsible persons of the locality who witness the IO's investigation at the scene of death. |
| 3 | **Panchnama** | The written report prepared by the IO in the presence of the Panchas describing the body, scene, and circumstances. |
| 4 | **Coroner** | A judicial officer (rank of First Class Magistrate) — could be a doctor or lawyer — who used to inquire into unnatural deaths under the old Coroner's Act (abolished in India). |
| 5 | **Exhumation** | Lawful disinterment of an already-buried body for further medicolegal examination. |
