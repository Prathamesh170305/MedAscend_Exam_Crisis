# LP-04 — Diagram-Specific Prerequisites

| # | Term | Plain meaning |
|---|------|----------------|
| 1 | **Documentary evidence** | Evidence in the form of written or recorded documents (certificates, MLRs, reports). |
| 2 | **Oral evidence** | Statements made by a witness in court under oath. |
| 3 | **Real (material) evidence** | Physical objects produced before the court (a weapon, clothing, viscera). |
| 4 | **Hearsay evidence** | Statements heard from another person about the matter — generally inadmissible, with specific exceptions. |
| 5 | **Circumstantial evidence** | Indirect evidence from which a fact can be inferred but not directly proved. |
