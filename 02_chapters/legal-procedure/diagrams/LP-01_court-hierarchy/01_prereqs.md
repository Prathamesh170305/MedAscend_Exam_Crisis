# LP-01 — Diagram-Specific Prerequisites

| # | Term | Plain meaning |
|---|------|----------------|
| 1 | **Jurisdiction** | The geographical area or subject matter over which a court has authority to hear cases. |
| 2 | **Sessions Court** | The highest criminal court at the district level — tries serious offences and can impose any sentence including death. |
| 3 | **Magistrate Court** | A lower criminal court (Metropolitan or Judicial) that tries less serious offences with a capped sentence. |
| 4 | **Capital punishment** | Death sentence — can be awarded only by a Sessions Court or higher, and must be confirmed by the High Court. |
| 5 | **Cognizance** | A court's act of formally taking up a case for trial. |
