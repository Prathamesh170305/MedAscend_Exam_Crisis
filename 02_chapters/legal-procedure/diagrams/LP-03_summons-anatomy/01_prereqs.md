# LP-03 — Diagram-Specific Prerequisites

| # | Term | Plain meaning |
|---|------|----------------|
| 1 | **Conduct money** | The travelling and subsistence allowance paid in advance to a witness summoned by the **civil court**. |
| 2 | **Witness summons** | A summons issued to a person directing him to appear in court and give evidence. |
| 3 | **Production summons** | A summons directing a person to produce specified documents or objects (e.g. medical records) in court. |
| 4 | **Court seal** | The official stamp of the court affixed to the summons, making it a legal order. |
