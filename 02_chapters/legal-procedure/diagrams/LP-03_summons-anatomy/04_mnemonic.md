# LP-03 — Mnemonic

🧠 **MNEMONIC — "4 W's of a Summons"**

| W | Stands for | What the summons states |
|---|------------|--------------------------|
| **Who** | Person summoned | Name + address of the witness/respondent |
| **When** | Date and time | Exact date + time of attendance |
| **Where** | Court + place | Court issuing the summons |
| **What** | Purpose | Give evidence (witness) OR produce documents (production) |

➕ **Conduct money rule:**
- 💰 **C**ivil case → **C**onduct money paid in advance
- ⚖️ **C**riminal case → No advance; government reimburses
