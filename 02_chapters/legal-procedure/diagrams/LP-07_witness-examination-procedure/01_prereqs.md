# LP-07 — Diagram-Specific Prerequisites

| # | Term | Plain meaning |
|---|------|----------------|
| 1 | **Examination-in-chief** | The first round of questioning, by the party that called the witness, to elicit facts that support its case. |
| 2 | **Cross-examination** | The second round of questioning, by the opposing party, to test the credibility of the witness. |
| 3 | **Re-examination** | A third round by the original calling party to clarify points raised in cross-examination — no new matter without leave. |
| 4 | **Leading question** | A question that suggests its own answer (e.g., "You were at the scene, weren't you?"). |
| 5 | **Court question** | A question put to the witness directly by the judge — can be asked at any stage. |
