**DT-1 — Civil Court vs Criminal Court**
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

| Feature | Civil Court | Criminal Court |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **Nature of dispute** | Private disputes (property, contract, family, tort) | Offences against the state (theft, hurt, murder, rape) |
| **Initiated by** | Plaint filed by the aggrieved party | FIR / complaint to police; state prosecutes |
| **Parties** | Plaintiff vs Defendant | State (prosecution) vs Accused |
| **Highest district court** | District Court / Court of Civil Judge | Sessions Court |
| **Object of trial** | Award compensation / decree | Punish the offender + protect society |
| **Burden of proof** | Preponderance of probability (balance of probabilities) | Proof **beyond reasonable doubt** |
| **Standard of evidence** | Lower | Higher |
| **Outcome** | Decree (monetary, injunction, declaration) | Sentence (imprisonment, fine, death) |
| **Conduct money** | Paid to witness in advance | Not paid in advance (govt reimburses after) |
| **Appeal** | District Court → High Court → Supreme Court | Magistrate → Sessions → High Court → Supreme Court |
| **Examples** | Land disputes, divorce, recovery of debt, medical-negligence damages claim | Murder, rape, grievous hurt, theft, criminal negligence |
