# DT-2 — MCQs (Cognizable vs Non-cognizable)

---

**Q1.** A cognizable offence is one in which:
A) Police can arrest without warrant
B) Police need a magistrate's warrant to arrest
C) Bail is automatically granted
D) Trial is in a civil court

🔓 Reveal Answer
✅ **Correct: A — Police can arrest without warrant**
💡 Cognizable = "able to be taken cognizance of by police directly". Serious offences (murder, rape, dacoity) fall here.
🏷️ Tag: `[UNIV]`

---

**Q2.** Which of the following is a cognizable offence?
A) Simple hurt
B) Defamation
C) Murder
D) Public nuisance

🔓 Reveal Answer
✅ **Correct: C — Murder**
💡 Murder is a heinous offence — police can arrest without warrant; trial in Sessions Court; non-bailable.
🏷️ Tag: `[UNIV]`

---

**Q3.** A non-cognizable offence requires:
A) No police involvement
B) Magistrate's order before police can investigate / arrest
C) Sessions Court trial
D) Coroner's inquest

🔓 Reveal Answer
✅ **Correct: B — Magistrate's order before police can investigate / arrest**
💡 In non-cognizable offences, police must obtain the magistrate's permission before investigating or arresting.
