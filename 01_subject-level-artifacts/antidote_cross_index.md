# 💊 Antidote Cross-Index (Toxicology)

**Subject-level pinned artefact (FMT).** Populated during the toxicology section. Every poison card auto-contributes its specific antidote (+ supportive measures) to this index.

**Columns:**
- **Poison** — exact card name + parent chapter
- **Specific antidote** — name, route, dose, mechanism
- **Supportive measures** — symptomatic treatment, decontamination
- **MLI** — medicolegal note (if any)
